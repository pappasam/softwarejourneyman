One-tool jobs

There is one right tool for every task in software. If there are seemingly many right tools, you may be suffering from:

  1. Subject matter ignorance
  2. Broad, non-specific requirements
  3. An enterprise integration for an organization systemically suffering from 1 and 2

As a software journeyman, your goal is to minimize your subject matter ignorance and clarify requirements. If you are working on an enterprise integration, reference Human error for tips on surviving the folly of man.

Posts in this section are dedicated to one specific job and its best corresponding tool.
